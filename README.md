## Key Principles (From psychology.md)

1. **Algorithm rewards emotion, not information**
   - Comments > Likes > Views
   - Make people FEEL something

2. **Information ≠ Provocation**
   - Don't just teach — make them feel it

3. **If readers feel "this is selling" — they scroll past**
   - Best content feels like a story, not a transaction

4. **The 6 Triggers**
   - Identity validation, Status signaling, Tribal belonging
   - Productive discomfort, Curiosity gap, Aspiration

5. **Authenticity filter**
   - Is this true? Would you say it in person?
   - Does this serve the reader or you?
