## AGENT INSTRUCTIONS (DETAILED)

### What coaching/PD audiences download
- **Self-assessment tools** — quizzes, reflection prompts, pattern-identifier checklists
- **Workbooks** — guided exercises they can do alone before committing to coaching
- **Mini-frameworks** — named systems that make a complex inner process feel navigable
- **Honest guides** — "what nobody tells you about X" style resources
- **Scripts** — for difficult conversations, boundary-setting, or self-advocacy

### Tone for coaching lead magnets
- Warmer than SaaS, more direct than pure therapy-speak
- The CTA must feel like an invitation, not a grab
- Avoid: "limited spots available" — this audience can smell desperation
- Prefer: "I made this because I wished it existed when I needed it"

### The coaching lead magnet CTA
Best performing CTAs in this niche:
1. "Comment [WORD] and I'll DM you the [resource name]"
2. "The link is in my bio / first comment" (lower friction)
3. "Send me a DM with [WORD] and I'll send it over"

## THE PSYCHOLOGY OF VIRAL LEAD MAGNETS

### Why Regular Lead Magnets Fail
Most lead magnets fail because they're built on the **value promise model**: "get my free guide to X." The problem? Everyone promises value, nobody believes it anymore. We've all downloaded "free guides" that were thinly-veiled sales pitches with zero actual insight.

### Investment Signaling: The New Model
The new model isn't about promising value — it's about *proving* value through visible effort.

When you say "I spent 27 hours creating this resource," you're proving you've invested real time. That creates an asymmetry that triggers reciprocity psychology. The reader thinks: "if they spent 27 hours on this, it must be good. And they're giving it away for free. I should at least check it out."

**Investment signaling examples:**
- "the 52-page workbook I created after 3 years of coaching clients through exactly this"
- "the exact script I used to have the conversation that changed everything"
- "the 3-day reflection journal I built for my $5k clients (now free)"

### The Lead Magnet Emotional Stack
The highest-performing lead magnets stack multiple emotional triggers:

1. **Curiosity Gap (hook)**
   "the exact conversation script that saved my most important relationship"

2. **Identity Validation (problem statement)**
   "if you've been avoiding the conversation you know you need to have, it's not because you're weak. It's because you've never had language for it that felt true."

3. **Tribal Belonging (us vs them)**
   "most 'self-help' teaches you to be positive. The people who actually transform their lives get specific. They get real."

4. **Investment Signaling (credibility)**
   "I've spent 5 years perfecting this script with 200+ coaching clients. Here's the version I wish existed when I was stuck."

5. **Aspiration + Believability (offer)**
   "I've turned it into a free script you can use tonight. Comment 'SCRIPT' and I'll send it over."

### The FOMO Stack
The psychological sequence that creates maximum lead magnet engagement:

1. **Social Proof + Specificity**
   "This has been requested 847 times. I'm releasing it to see what happens."

2. **Transformation Story**
   "One client used this script and finally had the conversation she'd been avoiding for 2 years. Another client said it changed how he sets boundaries at work."

3. **Exclusivity Signal**
   "I normally only share this inside my $3k program. But I want to test how it does in the wild."

4. **Action Urgency**
   "Comment 'SCRIPT' below and I'll DM it. Reading every response personally."

### The Lead Magnet CTA Psychology
For coaching/PD:
- Warm, invitation-style CTAs: "Comment 'YES' if this resonated"
- Avoid desperation scarcity — focus on exclusivity
- Connection + DM drives relationship building
- The "I made this because I wished it existed" framing resonates deeply
